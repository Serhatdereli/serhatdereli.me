# Weekly Money Review (15 minutes)

The habit that keeps you out of the hole once you're out of it. Same day, same
time, every week. Put it in your calendar as a recurring event (see
[`../reminders/calendar-and-email-setup.md`](../reminders/calendar-and-email-setup.md)).

Pick a low-stakes time — e.g. **Sunday 10:00** with a coffee. It's 15 minutes, not
an audit.

---

## The checklist

1. **Open the snapshot** ([`money-snapshot.md`](./money-snapshot.md)).
   - [ ] Update "cash available right now."
   - [ ] Any new income or bills since last week? Add them.

2. **Scan deadlines** (fines + debts/bills).
   - [ ] Anything due in the next 10 days? Mark it as this week's focus.
   - [ ] Any fine approaching its **discount deadline**? Handle it *now*.

3. **Process the brain dump.**
   - [ ] Anything new worrying you? Add it to the snapshot brain-dump list and
         give it a home (a row in fines or debts).

4. **Money actions taken this week** (write them down — momentum is motivating).
   - Paid: [...]
   - Contested / contacted creditor: [...]
   - Cancelled / paused: [...]

5. **Set this week's top 3.**
   1. [ ] [...]
   2. [ ] [...]
   3. [ ] [...]

6. **Close the laptop.** Done. You're now ahead of where you were last week.

---

## Monthly add-on (once a month, +10 min)

- [ ] Re-total everything owed — is it shrinking? (It will, even slowly.)
- [ ] Review flexible spend — anything to cut or restart?
- [ ] One "future-proofing" step: e.g. start a tiny buffer (even £5–10/week) so
      the *next* surprise fine doesn't become a crisis.
